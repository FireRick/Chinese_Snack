# Steps

## step 1:

Wash sweet potatoes.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Cut it in half and steamed in a steamer.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Easy to puncture with chopsticks is ripe.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Natural air to warm skinning.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Then cool and cut into thumb-thick, long and short fries.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Place the chips in a bamboo dustpan.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Cover the net and bask in the sun for 2 to 3 days.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/7.jpg)

## step 8:

Finished products.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/419443/8.jpg)
