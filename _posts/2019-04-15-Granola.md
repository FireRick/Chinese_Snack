# Granola

![Granola]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/452034.jpg)

# Introduction

**"Granola with oat, nuts and fruits, crisp and delicious, healthy and delicious"**

# Materials


## Main material

Material|Quantity
--|--
Original oatmeal|150g
Corn flakes|30g

## accessories

Material|Quantity
--|--
Nut|100g (hazelnut, walnut, peanut)
Preserved fruit|100g (dried mango, strawberry, yellow peach, sour cherry)
Vegetable oil|1 tablespoons
Brown sugar|1 tablespoons
Maple syrup|3 tablespoons
Honey|2 tablespoons

# Characteristics

Property|--
--|--
Flavor|Sweet taste
Technology|roast
time consuming|An hour
difficulty|ordinary

# Steps

## step 1:

Preheat oven 150 degrees.Weigh the original oatmeal.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Prepare nuts, pour them into thick transparent bags, add a layer of bags outside, and use a rolling pin or glass bottle to crush the nuts for reserve.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Prepare dried yellow peach.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Dried diced yellow peach.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Prepare dried mango.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Dried mango diced.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Prepare dried strawberries.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/7.jpg)

## step 8:

Dried diced strawberries.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/8.jpg)

## step 9:

Prepare dried sour cherries.

![step 9]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/9.jpg)

## step 10:

Dry diced sour cherry.

![step 10]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/10.jpg)

## step 11:

Mix the dried fruit.

![step 11]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/11.jpg)

## step 12:

Prepare cornflakes.

![step 12]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/12.jpg)

## step 13:

Prepare a large bowl and mix all ingredients except dried fruit and cornflakes.

![step 13]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/13.jpg)

## step 14:

Mix the ingredients well.

![step 14]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/14.jpg)

## step 15:

Paper the baking tray and smooth the mixture.

![step 15]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/15.jpg)

## step 16:

Bake in oven, 150 degrees, 30 minutes.Remove and mix every 10 minutes.

![step 16]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/16.jpg)

## step 17:

Take it out as soon as the time is up.

![step 17]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/17.jpg)

## step 18:

Immediately mix in cornflakes and dried fruits.

![step 18]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/18.jpg)

## step 19:

Finished products.

![step 19]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/19.jpg)

## step 20:

Crisp and delicious, rich in layers.

![step 20]({{site.baseurl}}/img/452034/20.jpg)
