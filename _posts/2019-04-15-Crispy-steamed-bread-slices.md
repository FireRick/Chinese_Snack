# Steps

## step 1:

Steamed bread slices.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401264/1.jpg)

## step 2:

## step 3:

Salt and thirteen fragrances.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401264/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Disperse with chopsticks.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401264/4.jpg)
