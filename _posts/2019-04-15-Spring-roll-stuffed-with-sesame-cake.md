# Spring roll stuffed with sesame cake

![Spring roll stuffed with sesame cake]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/420609.jpg)

# Materials


## Main material

Material|Quantity
--|--
spring roll wrapper|12 sheets
Cake|7 pieces
Egg|2 only

## accessories

Material|Quantity
--|--
White sesame seeds|Appropriate amount
Semen Sesami nigrum|Appropriate amount

# Characteristics

Property|--
--|--
Flavor|Sweet taste
Technology|Deep fried
time consuming|Ten minutes
difficulty|ordinary

# Steps

## step 1:

Ingredients: Spring roll skin.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Cake.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Eggs (beaten into egg juice).

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Cut the cake with scissors and put it in a big bowl.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Then, mix in black and white sesame seeds, pour in egg mixture and mix well to form sesame cake filling.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Then, take the spring roll skin and put it on the board and put the sesame cake filling on it.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Wrap it up.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/7.jpg)

## step 8:

All wrapped spring rolls.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/8.jpg)

## step 9:

Finally, put the spring rolls into the oil pan and fry them in medium and small heat until they are golden.

![step 9]({{site.baseurl}}/img/420609/9.jpg)
