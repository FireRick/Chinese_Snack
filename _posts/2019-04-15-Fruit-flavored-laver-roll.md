# Steps

## step 1:

Mix rice with vinegar, sugar, carrots and cucumbers.The sushi curtain is covered with a fresh-keeping film larger than laver.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Spread the rice on the plastic wrap.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Spread a laver on the rice and a layer of rice.Put carrot and Cucumber Strips on the bottom and squeeze a strawberry jam.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Preservation film and bamboo curtain are pinched together, and the bamboo curtain is rolled up tightly.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Remove fresh-keeping film and bamboo curtain while rolling.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/5.jpg)

## step 6:

After rolling and pressing, open the fresh-keeping film and bamboo curtain.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Sprinkle coconut on the rice layer and the preservation film, and wrap the preservation film again to make the coconut stick evenly on the rice layer.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/7.jpg)

## step 8:

Finished product drawing.Cut the rice roll into even pieces with a knife, then squeeze some strawberry jam on the surface.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413068/8.jpg)
