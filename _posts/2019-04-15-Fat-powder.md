# Steps

## step 1:

Remove the root of the fresh bean sprouts and wash them for later use.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Dried sweet potato flour is soaked in boiling water for about 20 minutes.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Previously, sauerkraut was cooked as part of the large intestine.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Boil water in a pot, pour it into the large intestine and continue to cook for ten minutes.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Cook the bean sprouts and fish them into a bowl.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/5.jpg)

## step 6:

A seasoning made of self-made chili oil, chicken essence, sugar, sesame oil, vinegar, raw soy sauce and scallion beforehand.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Put the soaked sweet potato noodles into the pot and boil them.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/7.jpg)

## step 8:

When boiled, it can be easily clamped with chopsticks to turn off the fire.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/8.jpg)

## step 9:

Sprinkle onions and coriander in a bowl, and the sausage powder with full color, fragrance and flavor will be ready.

![step 9]({{site.baseurl}}/img/401821/9.jpg)

# Tips

Dried sweet potato powder can be bought in supermarkets or online.Like to eat hemp can put pepper powder or pepper oil.If you don't like acid, you don't need vinegar.
