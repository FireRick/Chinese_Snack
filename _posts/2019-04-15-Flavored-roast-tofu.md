# Materials


## Main material

Material|Quantity
--|--
Bean curd|1 pieces
Cumin powder|5 grams
Cayenne pepper|3 grams
salt|5 grams
Soy sauce|5 grams
Old soy sauce|5 grams
Edible oil|16 grams
Honey|5 grams
Cooked white sesame|Appropriate amount

## accessories

Material|Quantity
--|--
Chopped green onion|Few

Property|--

# Steps

## step 1:

Prepare the necessary ingredients.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Pour cumin powder, chili powder, salt, sauce, old sauce, cooking oil and honey into a bowl and mix well.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Cut tofu into blocks of the same thickness. Shallowly scratch the tofu with a knife tip for 2 to 3 times on both sides. Place them in a baking pan with tin paper in turn.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Brush the tofu with sauce and put it in the preheated oven.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Bake at 210 degrees for 5 minutes.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Then take out the dough and rinse with sauce, and bake in the oven for 10 minutes. Sprinkle white sesame and onion immediately after baking.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/6.jpg)

## step 7:

The fragrance is delicious!

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/433934/7.jpg)
