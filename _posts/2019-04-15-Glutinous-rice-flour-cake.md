# Glutinous rice flour cake

![Glutinous rice flour cake]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/446412.jpg)

Material|Quantity

Sugar|Appropriate amount

# Steps

## step 1:

Stir glutinous rice flour with boiling water and chopsticks to form flocculent dough.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Mix the ripe black sesame, the ripe peanuts and sugar together.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Divide the dough into small doses.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Sprinkle some dry glutinous rice flour on the board, take a prescription and flatten it into skin with your hands.Don't be too thin.Fill in the stuffing.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Put a little oil in the non-stick pan, fry the cake flat in the pan until golden brown on both sides, and the cake expands.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Rinse brown sugar into brown sugar water, about half a cup of water, and pour into the pot.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Boil until juice is collected.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/446412/7.jpg)
