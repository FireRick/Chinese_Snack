# Steps

## step 1:

Put all the powder in the bowl, add yeast and white sesame, and add oil.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Stir in water to form a thick paste.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Cover the film and ferment for a while.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Just a semi-fermented look.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/4.jpg)

## step 5:

The wonton skin is slightly elongated.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Blow it up.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Soak lettuce in salt water and set aside.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/7.jpg)

## step 8:

Make your own sausage and fry it.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/8.jpg)

## step 9:

Spread the batter in a pan, thinner.

![step 9]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/9.jpg)

## step 10:

Beat in the eggs and sprinkle with onions.

![step 10]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/10.jpg)

## step 11:

Brush sweet sauce and chili oil and put in the prepared ingredients.

![step 11]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/11.jpg)

## step 12:

Just put lettuce rolls on.

![step 12]({{site.baseurl}}/img/410769/12.jpg)
