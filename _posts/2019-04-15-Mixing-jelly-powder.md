# Mixing jelly powder

![Mixing jelly powder]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/395886.jpg)

# Introduction

**"Shopping in the morning market, I found that there were some cooked mungbean powder sold, so I bought a piece and mixed it at noon. It's just suitable for summer."**

# Steps

## step 1:

One piece of cold powder, rinse the skin, remove the surface floating ash.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Cut the cold powder into bars and put it on the plate.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Onion, minced.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Garlic, minced.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Sauce, add sugar, salt, vinegar, soy sauce, chili oil, onion, garlic, mix well, let stand for 10 minutes, and taste well.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Prepared sauce, sprinkle on the cold powder, mix well when eating, then.Eat in summer. It's delicious.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/395886/6.jpg)
