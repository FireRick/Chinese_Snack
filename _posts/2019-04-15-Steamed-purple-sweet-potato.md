# Steps

## step 1:

Wash purple potatoes.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/404129/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Cut purple potatoes in half and put them in a steaming pot. Steam over high heat for 20 minutes.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/404129/2.jpg)

Steamed purple potatoes.
