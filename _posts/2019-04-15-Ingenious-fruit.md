# Steps

## step 1:

Wash and drain fresh pork and cut it into pieces in a non-stick pan. Stir-fry until crispy meat is fried until oil is released.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/1.jpg)

## step 3:

Put the flour in the container and pour in four eggs.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Add sugar powder and pour into pig oil. Add proper amount of water and evenly spread it into dough. Cover with damp cloth and wake up to 30 minutes.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/4.jpg)

## step 5:

The wake-up dough is kneaded on the panel to form a delicate and smooth reserve.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Take out a piece of dough and knead it into a thin strip to prepare the fruit mould.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Sprinkle the flour in the mould first to prevent the dough from sticking in the mould, then press the strip dough into the mould and press the excess dough down with a knife blade, then compact it, and the dough can be rethought out.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/7.jpg)

## step 8:

After 20 minutes of waking up, put the cake in the oven and heat it up and down for 10 minutes, then turn off the fire, leave it on and fry it for 5 minutes.

![step 8]({{site.baseurl}}/img/413958/8.jpg)
