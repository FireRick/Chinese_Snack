# Steps

## step 1:

Wash and drain pork.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/1.jpg)

## step 2:

After slicing, gently pat the flabby slices with a rolling pin.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Mix sliced garlic with meat and seasoning and marinate.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Coat the cured meat slices with dry flour.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Prepare meat slices as shown.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/5.jpg)

## step 6:

Mother Goose's Jingdian pot is heated. Add a little oil to the slices and start frying the steak.

![step 6]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/6.jpg)

## step 7:

Fry until golden brown on both sides, slice and prepare pork chops dipped in sauce.

![step 7]({{site.baseurl}}/img/402520/7.jpg)
