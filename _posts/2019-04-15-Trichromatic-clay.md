# Steps

## step 1:

Pumpkin and tomatoes peeled and sliced and steamed in a pot.

![step 1]({{site.baseurl}}/img/406779/1.jpg)

## step 2:

Cut the bean skin into pieces.

![step 2]({{site.baseurl}}/img/406779/2.jpg)

## step 3:

Steamed pumpkins and tomatoes are mashed with rolling sticks.

![step 3]({{site.baseurl}}/img/406779/3.jpg)

## step 4:

Look, you have appetite.

![step 4]({{site.baseurl}}/img/406779/4.jpg)

## step 5:

Add chopped soybean skin.

![step 5]({{site.baseurl}}/img/406779/5.jpg)
